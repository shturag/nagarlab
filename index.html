Who We Are

NagarLab is led by a Data Scientist in Public Service and supported by a technical team. We build practical, rapid, and secure data/AI systems so government leaders can act early, allocate better, and improve service quality with confidence.

What We Deliver

  • Operational dashboards for public sectors, private sectors, and agencies
  • Live citizen sentiment and policy response tracking
  • Signal detection for misinformation and social risk
  • Diaspora expertise matching for national priorities
